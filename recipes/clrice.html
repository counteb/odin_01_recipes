Cilantro Lime Rice

Description

A medley of flavors combine to make a fresh herby buttery rice dish that will elevate any meal.

Ingredients

  • Basmati rice X 1 cup
  • Bunch of cilantro
  • Salt
  • Garlic X 1/2 head
  • Butter 1/4 cup
  • Water X 2 cups

Recipe

  1. Gently rinse the rice 3 times
  2. Place the rice in a pot with water and a pinch of salt
  3. Bring to a boil, cover and cook for ten minuets
  4. Chop up cilantro and garlic
  5. After the rice has sat off heat for 10 minuets add garlic and cilantro
  6. Cube butter and add to rice
  7. Salt to taste
  8. Gently mix
  9. Enjoy
