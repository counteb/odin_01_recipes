Hummus

Description

Using dry beans and fresh ingredients this recipe will take your hummus to a level smoothness and flavor you never knew existed.

Ingredients

  • Dry garbanzo beans X 1lb
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Extra virgin olive oil X 1/2 cup
  • Lemon Juice X 1 lemon
  • Garlic X 6 cloves
  • Tahini X 1tsp

Recipe

  1. Cover with water and soak the garbanzo beans over night
  2. Drain and rinse the beans
  3. Place in a pot and cover with water
  4. Boil the beans till tender
  5. Drain and either add beans to a mixer or blender or a bowel you can mash them in
  6. Mash or mix the beans with 1tsp salt add more to taste
  7. Grate or chop the garlic
  8. add the rest of the ingredients with 2tbl of water
  9. Taste and add extra of anything to balance the flavors
  10. Enjoy
